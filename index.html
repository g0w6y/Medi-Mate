Medi-Mate: Connected Care

Revolutionizing elder care in India with an offline-first, voice-enabled medication management platform that connects elders, caretakers, and families.

Explore Features View Impact

The Problem We're Solving

70% of Indian elders miss or delay medicines regularly. Care is fragmented: elders forget, caretakers stressed, families anxious. Existing apps fail India → no offline-first, no local language, no SOS.

Target Users & Pain Points

The pain points demand a connected solution.

Elders (70%)

Forget meds, fear emergencies, tech struggle.

Caretakers (60%)

Overloaded, no structured accountability.

Families (65%)

Constant worry, no real-time updates.

Doctors (30-40%)

Need clean adherence & vitals data.

Feature Overview

💊 Medication & Health

  • Smart med reminders & low stock alerts
  • Weekly adherence/vitals chart
  • AI health reading & quick assistance

👵 Elder-Friendly Design

  • Voice notifications in regional languages
  • Simple UI with large buttons
  • Dark/light mode for comfort

👥 Caretaker & Family

  • Caretaker task lists + confirmations
  • Family chat & real-time notifications
  • SOS alerts & care concern reporting

🚀 Extra Innovations

  • Auto CSV health reports
  • Smart retry reminders with escalation
  • Geo-tagged caretaker check-ins

How It Works

Smart Reminders

  • Voice + visual alerts in local languages
  • Simple confirm: "Taken / Missed"
  • Weekly adherence chart auto-updated

Connected Care

  • Caretaker sees instant updates
  • Family gets real-time notifications
  • Auto CSV health reports shared weekly

Safety First

  • One-tap SOS button
  • Alerts caretaker + family instantly
  • Emergency auto-call option to saved contacts

Impact

Elders

Voice-based reminders in their own language, higher medication adherence → fewer emergencies, peace of mind.

Caretakers

Organized daily task lists, less chance of missing or double-dosing, clear communication with family → reduced blame/stress.

Families

Instant updates if medicine is missed, weekly reports → better visibility, reduced anxiety.

Doctors

Accurate weekly logs of meds & vitals, saves time, supports better, data-driven treatment decisions.

"Medi-Mate isn't just about taking medicines on time — it's about giving elders dignity, families peace, and India a healthier future."
